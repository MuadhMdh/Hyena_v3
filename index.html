Welcome to Hyena.v3

The R15 V3, affectionately named Hyena, is a 155cc sport bike crafted for those who crave both speed and style. Cloaked in a striking gray with gold accents, Hyena has a look that matches its performance — fierce and unforgettable. This machine is built to handle the road with agility, reaching impressive top speeds over 155 km/h. With its sporty design and powerful engine, Hyena is more than just a bike; it’s an invitation to experience the thrill of the open road and the adrenaline of true speed.
